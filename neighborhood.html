Get In the Snow

Winter in MinneSNOWta isn't complete without some snowy activities! Located only 30mins from downtown Minneapolis, is Buck Hill . A small ski hill great for families or beginner to moderate skiers looking for a day on the slopes. This hill has seen some talent - this is where Olympian Lindsey Vonn got her start!
